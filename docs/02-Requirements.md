# 02 - Requirements

## Overview
QtyWise is a smart inventory management and quantity optimization platform.

## Functional Requirements
- **Inventory Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of stock levels across multiple locations.
- **Quantity Optimization**: Suggesting optimal order quantities based on historic sales, lead times, and seasonal trends.
- **Supplier Management**: Track supplier catalogs, lead times, contact details, and performance.
- **Reporting & Analytics**: Dashboards showing inventory turnover, stockouts, and capital tied up.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- **Performance**: Page load times under 2 seconds.
- **Scalability**: Capable of handling up to 100,000 SKUs.
- **Security**: Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) and encrypted database connections.
